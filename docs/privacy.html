Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

No personal information is collected.

TextStar is a Mac writing app for working with your own files. TextStar does not collect personal information, track your writing, sell data, share data, or use your documents for analytics or advertising.

Your writing stays with you.

TextStar saves your work to files you control. The app does not upload your documents to TextStar servers and does not provide cloud collaboration, remote sync, AI processing, or account-based services.

No analytics or tracking.

TextStar does not include third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or behavior tracking scripts.

Support emails.

If you email support, the information you choose to send will be used only to respond to your request.

Contact.

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@gettextstar.com.
